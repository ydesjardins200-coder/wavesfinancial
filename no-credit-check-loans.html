Our approval model

We look at your income. Full stop.

Traditional lenders obsess over your credit report. We built a completely different system — one that focuses on your current ability to repay, not your financial past.

💰
Consistent income of at least $800/month net
Employment, self-employment, CPP, EI, disability, or other regular income all qualify. We just need to see it in your bank statement history.
✓ We check this

An active Canadian bank account
Any active Canadian chequing or savings account works. We use Flinks — a read-only, bank-grade verification tool — to verify it securely.
✓ We check this

No excessive NSF transactions recently
A few NSFs won't disqualify you — everyone has rough patches. We look for a pattern of stability over the last 90 days, not perfection.
✓ We check this

No active bankruptcy or consumer proposal
If you currently have an active (undischarged) bankruptcy or consumer proposal, lending law prevents us from extending credit. Discharged? You may still qualify.

A note on responsible borrowing

Waves Financial is committed to responsible lending. Our loans carry a 23% annual percentage rate and are designed for short-term needs — not as a long-term financial solution. We encourage every applicant to borrow only what they can comfortably repay. If you are facing significant debt challenges, free resources like the Financial Consumer Agency of Canada and non-profit credit counselling services can help. We are here to help in a tough moment — not to make things harder.
