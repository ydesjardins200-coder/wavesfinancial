Step-by-step guide

How to send your e-Transfer payment

1
Log into your online or mobile banking
Open your bank's app or website. This works with all major Canadian banks — TD, RBC, BMO, Scotiabank, CIBC, Tangerine, Simplii, EQ Bank, and most credit unions.
2
Find the Interac e-Transfer section
Look for "Send e-Transfer," "Interac e-Transfer," or "Transfer Money" in your banking app. The exact label varies by bank but the function is the same.
3
Add Waves Financial as a recipient
Enter our payment email address as the recipient. You only need to do this once — your bank will save it for future payments.
paiement@wavesfinancial.ca
4
Enter the amount and add your reference
Enter your payment amount. In the message field, include your loan reference number (found in your loan agreement email) so we can match the payment instantly.
5
Submit and confirm below
Send the transfer in your banking app, then fill out the confirmation form on this page. This lets our team apply your payment to your account immediately.
