FAQ

Questions answered

No. Waves Financial does not require a credit check to get approved. We assess your eligibility based on your current income and ability to repay. Whether you have bad credit, no credit history, or past financial setbacks — you're welcome to apply.
Once approved and your digital agreement is signed, funds are sent via Interac e-Transfer — typically within 2 minutes. We operate 24/7, including evenings, weekends, and holidays.
We currently serve residents of British Columbia, Ontario, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, and Newfoundland & Labrador. We are working to expand to additional provinces soon.
Repayments are made through pre-authorized debits from your bank account. You choose your payment frequency — weekly, biweekly, semi-monthly, or monthly — and we automatically collect the scheduled amount. You can repay early with no penalty.
You must be at least 18 years old, a resident of a province we serve, have a steady source of income for the past 3 months, a net monthly income of at least $800, and an active Canadian bank account that supports pre-authorized debits.
