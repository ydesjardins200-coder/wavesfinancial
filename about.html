Our story

Where Waves Financial came from

The founders of Waves Financial had all faced the same wall at some point: a sudden expense — a car repair, a medical bill, a gap before payday — and nowhere reasonable to turn. Banks said no. Credit cards were maxed. The only options left were predatory payday lenders with triple-digit effective rates and hidden fees.

They weren't alone. Millions of Canadians face this exact situation every year — not because they're irresponsible, but because emergencies don't wait for the right moment and traditional lending has never been designed to move fast or serve people with imperfect credit histories.

Waves Financial was built to close that gap. A lender that moves at the speed of an emergency, charges a fair and clearly disclosed rate, never checks your credit score, and is staffed by real Canadians who treat you like an adult.

We're not a faceless institution. We're a team that genuinely believes the financial system should work for regular Canadians — and we built this company as proof that it can.

What drives us

Our mission and the values behind it

Our mission
To give every Canadian access to fast, fair, and fully transparent short-term credit — regardless of their credit history — and to do it with the honesty and respect every person deserves.
We measure success not just in loans funded, but in the number of Canadians who feel genuinely helped — not trapped, not judged, not taken advantage of. Every product decision, every rate, every support interaction is held against that standard.
🔍
Radical transparency
Every fee, rate, and term is disclosed before you sign — in plain language, not legal jargon. No surprise charges. No buried conditions. Ever.
⚡
Speed as respect
When someone needs emergency funds, making them wait is a form of disrespect. Our ~2 minute e-Transfer is a values statement, not just a feature.
🤝
No judgment — ever
Financial difficulty can happen to anyone. Our team approaches every interaction without judgment, without lectures, and with genuine empathy.
🇨🇦
Proudly Canadian
Our team, our licensing, our operations — all Canadian. We understand the unique financial realities of Canadians because we live them too.
📐
Responsible by design
We only lend what borrowers can reasonably repay. Our repayment schedules are built to help, not to trap. We'd rather lose a sale than harm a customer.
The people behind Waves

A small team with a big commitment

Waves Financial is a deliberately small team. We believe that staying lean keeps us honest — every person on our team touches the product, the customer experience, or the operations directly. Nobody is insulated from the reality of what we do.

Our team spans lending compliance, technology, customer support, and finance. What unites everyone is a shared frustration with how traditional lending has failed regular Canadians, and a genuine belief that we can do better.

We're not backed by a distant institutional investor with no skin in the game. We're a team that built this because we believed it needed to exist — and we hold ourselves accountable to that every day.

What we promise

Our commitments to every Canadian we serve

📋
Full cost disclosure — always
Before you sign anything, you will see your exact APR, total interest charge, payment schedule, and total repayment amount. No exceptions. No surprises after the fact.
🚫
No credit check — never, ever
We will never access your credit report. Not a hard inquiry, not a soft one. Our income-based model means your past doesn't define your access to help today.
⚡
~2 minute e-Transfer, 24/7
Once approved and signed, your funds are on their way in approximately 2 minutes — any time of day, any day of the year. We built our system around this promise.
🔓
No lock-in — pay early, free
There is no prepayment penalty. If you want to pay off your loan before the scheduled end date, you pay only the interest accrued to that day. Not a dollar more.
🛡️
Your data stays yours
We never sell, share, or license your personal or banking information to third parties. 256-bit SSL encryption protects everything from application to final payment.
🤝
Responsible lending — always
We will never approve a loan we believe a borrower cannot reasonably repay. Our goal is to help — not to create a cycle of debt. If borrowing isn't right for your situation, we'll say so.
