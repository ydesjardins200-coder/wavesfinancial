Section 03
Eligibility Requirements
Plain language To qualify you need to be 18+, a Canadian resident in one of our 6 provinces, have a steady income of at least $800/month, and an active Canadian bank account. We never check your credit score.

To be eligible for a loan from Waves Financial, applicants must meet all of the following criteria at the time of application:

  • Be at least 18 years of age (or the age of majority in your province, whichever is greater)
  • Be a Canadian resident residing in British Columbia, Ontario, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, or Newfoundland and Labrador
  • Have a net income of at least $800 per month from employment, self-employment, government benefits (CPP, EI, disability), or other regular income sources
  • Hold an active Canadian chequing or savings account supporting Interac e-Transfer and Pre-Authorized Debit
  • Not currently be subject to an active (undischarged) bankruptcy or consumer proposal under the Bankruptcy and Insolvency Act
  • Provide accurate and complete information in your application

Waves Financial does not perform credit bureau inquiries of any kind — no hard inquiry, no soft inquiry. Approval is based solely on income verification via our secure banking verification partner. Meeting eligibility criteria does not guarantee loan approval. Waves Financial reserves the right to decline any application at its sole discretion.

Section 07
Repayment, Default & Collections
Plain language Payments are collected automatically from your bank on your scheduled dates. If a payment fails, contact us immediately — we'd rather find a solution than escalate. Persistent non-payment may result in collections.

Pre-Authorized Debit (PAD): By signing your Loan Agreement, you authorize Waves Financial to collect scheduled loan payments via Pre-Authorized Debit from the bank account you have designated. Payments will be collected on the dates specified in your repayment schedule.

NSF and returned payments: If a scheduled PAD is returned due to insufficient funds, an NSF fee (as specified in your Loan Agreement) may be applied to your account. Waves Financial strongly encourages borrowers to ensure adequate funds are available on each scheduled payment date.

Missed payments: If you anticipate difficulty making a scheduled payment, please contact us as soon as possible at 1-800-123-4567 or support@wavesfinancial.ca. We will work with you to find a solution, which may include a payment deferral or modified schedule, subject to eligibility.

Default: You are considered in default if you fail to make two or more consecutive scheduled payments without an approved arrangement. In the event of default, the remaining balance of your loan, including accrued interest, becomes due immediately.

Collections: In the event of default, Waves Financial may refer your account to a third-party collection agency or take legal action to recover the outstanding balance. All collection activities are conducted in accordance with applicable provincial collection agency legislation and the cost of collection may be added to the outstanding balance as permitted by law.

Early repayment: You may repay your loan in full at any time without penalty. To make an early repayment, send an Interac e-Transfer to paiement@wavesfinancial.ca and complete the confirmation form at wavesfinancial.ca/online-payment.
