Your security is our priority

Protecting you at every step

From the moment you start your application to the last repayment, your data is protected by industry-leading security standards.

🔐
256-Bit SSL Encryption

Every piece of data you submit — including your SIN, banking details, and personal information — is encrypted using the same 256-bit SSL standard used by major Canadian banks.

🏦
Read-Only Bank Verification

Our Flinks IBV integration connects to your bank in read-only mode. We can see your account activity to verify income — but we cannot initiate or move any funds outside of your pre-authorized repayment schedule.

🚫
Your Data Is Never Sold

We collect your information to process your loan application and manage your account — nothing more. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties or advertisers.

📜
PIPEDA Compliant

Waves Financial is fully compliant with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), giving you the right to access and correct your personal information at any time.

🛡️
Trusted & Regulated

Waves Financial is a licensed lender regulated in every province we serve. You're always protected by provincial consumer lending laws.
