Eligibility Requirements

Do you qualify?

Our eligibility criteria are straightforward. Most Canadians with a steady income qualify — regardless of their credit history.

🎂
Age 18 or Older

You must be at least 18 years old to apply. Residents of some provinces must be 19 or older.

🇨🇦
Canadian Resident

Currently available to residents of British Columbia, Ontario, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, and Newfoundland & Labrador.

💼
Steady Income — 3+ Months

You must have received the same source of income for at least the past 3 months. Employment, self-employment, government benefits, and EI all qualify.

💰
Minimum $800/Month Net

Your after-tax monthly income must be at least $800 to ensure you can comfortably manage repayments alongside your existing expenses.

🏦
Active Canadian Bank Account

You need an active Canadian chequing or savings account that supports pre-authorized debits, open for at least 3 months.

📵
No Active Bankruptcy

You must not be currently in bankruptcy or actively pursuing a consumer proposal at the time of your application.

Repayment

Flexible repayment options

Choose the repayment schedule that works best for your pay cycle. All payments are collected automatically via pre-authorized debit — no manual transfers, no missed payments.

📅
Weekly

Smaller payments every week. Ideal if you're paid weekly and prefer spreading your repayment over more installments.

📆
Every 2 Weeks (Biweekly)

The most popular option for Canadians. Aligns with most biweekly pay cycles for seamless automatic repayments.

🗓️
Twice a Month (Semi-Monthly)

Two fixed payments per month, typically on the 1st and 15th. Great for salaried employees paid semi-monthly.

📋
Monthly

One larger payment per month. Best for those on monthly income like pensions, government benefits, or self-employment.
